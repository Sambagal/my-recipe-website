1.Fluffy Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mash banana in a bowl
  2. Add egg, milk and mix well
  3. In another bowl, mix flour,sugar,baking powder and salt
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  5. Pour butter onto a greased hot pan
  6. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook the other side
  7. Serve warm with honey or syrup

2.Fresh Veggie Salad Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lettuce or spinach
  • 1/2 cucumber(sliced)
  • 1/2 ovacado(sliced)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes(halved)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, add all chopped vegetables
  2. In a small cup mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper
  3. Pour dressing over salad
  4. Toss well and serve fresh
